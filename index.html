Program Snapshot

20 days • Ages 15–25

This single-file website compiles the complete program framework you supplied: measurement systems (21 metrics), tools, schedule (5-day online pre‑bootcamp + 15‑day offline bootcamp), optimization actions, KPI formulas and sources. Everything has been arranged for readability and implementation.

Core Goals

  • Days 1–5 (Online): Clarify and finalize ideas; set build roadmaps.
  • Days 6–20 (Offline): Build from idea to usable MVP; validate with customers; aim for early revenue.
  • Outcomes: Functional MVPs, early revenue (target Rs 5,000+ per team), investor-ready demo day.

Innovative Differentiators

  • Real-time building: experts build alongside participants (no lecture-only sessions).
  • Market immersion: daily customer interactions, pop-up sales booths, fieldwork.
  • Micro-investment grants: Rs 10,000 micro-grant to top teams (Day 10).
  • Alumni mentor integration, gamified sprints and leaderboards.

Program DNA (Daily Pattern)

  • Morning — expert sessions & hands-on workshops
  • Afternoon — build sprints & customer outreach
  • Evening — mentor clinics & peer feedback
  • Night — optional late sprints, screenings & fireside chats

Program Optimization & Risk Management

18 strategies

Real-time Adjustments

  • Daily metric review sessions at 8 PM (Trigger: APR <85% or BVS <3). Expected: Maintain >90% engagement.
  • Immediate intervention protocols for underperformers (PSS <60 for 2 consecutive days) — recovery within 4 hours.
  • Dynamic curriculum adjustments next day if team TCS <3.5 or LAR <70%.

Weekly Optimization

  • Weekly mentor feedback integration if MEQ <4.0 or satisfaction <80%.
  • Resource reallocation when utilization >95%.
  • Success pattern identification and replication for top performers.

Resource Allocation

  • Maintain mentor-to-participant ratio 1:4 (check weekly).
  • Technical resource allocation based on complexity score >8.
  • Expert time allocation optimization monthly.

Quality Assurance

  • Peer review quality controls, daily checks if consistency <70%.
  • Customer feedback validation process if quality score <3.5.
  • MVP QA checkpoints scheduled to ensure completeness.

Scalability & Risk

  • Regional adaptation frameworks if variance >20% across locations.
  • Backup mentor assignment within 2 hours for unavailability >24 hours.
  • Emergency technical response within 30 minutes for critical downtime.
