Indian Entrepreneurs Winter Accelerator

7-Day Online Prep + 7-Day Offline Build + 1-Day Grand Pitch

Program Overview

Duration: 15 days total (7 days online pre-course + 7 days intensive offline + 1 Pitch Day)

Dates: December 2025

Audience: Indian entrepreneurs aged 15–25 ready to build, validate, and launch ideas fast.

Program Flow:
1. 7-Day Online Bootcamp (Basics, Mindset, and Market Readiness)
2. 7-Day Offline Accelerator (Intense Build, Real Validation, and Early Revenue)
3. Day 15: Pitch Day Extravaganza — surprise celebrity guests and real investors.

Online Bootcamp (7 Days)

Goal: Prepare participants with the right knowledge, frameworks, and clarity to hit the ground running.

DayThemeActivities
1Entrepreneurial MindsetSelf-assessment, storytelling, understanding the founder’s journey
2Idea GenerationWorkshops on problem-solving, opportunity spotting, and trend analysis
3Design ThinkingEmpathy maps, customer journey exploration, and idea prototyping
4Market ResearchCustomer interviews, validation strategies, and feedback loops
5Business FundamentalsUnit economics, lean canvas, and startup accounting basics
6Pitch ReadinessElevator pitch crafting, storytelling, and communication drills
7Pre-Build PrepFinalize MVP idea, build team synergy, and define clear 7-day build goals

Offline Accelerator (7 Days)

Goal: Move from validated idea → MVP → market validation → early traction within one week.

DayThemeActivities
1Kickoff & Team FormationFinal team setup, icebreakers, and rapid roadmap planning
2Prototype SprintBuild first MVP drafts; mentors code and design alongside teams
3User TestingCustomer interviews, live demos, and data collection
4Iteration MarathonRefine product using real feedback, speed challenges, peer reviews
5Monetization & SalesLaunch micro-sales booths, marketing experiments, revenue sprints
6Growth & OperationsBuild automation, optimize cost structure, test scalability
7Final Build DayAll-night hackathon, feature finalization, and investor pitch rehearsal

Pitch Day (Day 15)

The grand finale — teams showcase their startups before a live panel of investors, media, and surprise celebrity entrepreneurs.

  • Top 3 teams get direct mentorship + seed micro-grants
  • Media coverage + networking mixer with investors
  • Special guests from startup and entertainment ecosystems

Measurement & Optimization Framework

The same advanced quantitative framework continues to ensure quality and real-time improvement, including metrics like:

  • Active Participation Rate (APR) >90%
  • Build Velocity Score (BVS) ≥4.0
  • MVP Progress Tracker >80%
  • Revenue Generation Index (Rs 5,000+ per team)
  • Program Success Index >80

Real-time dashboards, mentor evaluations, and peer assessments guide daily optimization and ensure the best outcomes.
